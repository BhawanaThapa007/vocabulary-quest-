📚 Learn the Words
Read each word, listen to pronunciation, and study the meaning.
Achieve
/ə-CHEEV/
To successfully reach a goal through effort.
"She worked hard to achieve her dream."
Diligent
/DIL-ih-jent/
Showing careful and persistent effort.
"He was diligent in finishing his homework."
Excel
/ek-SEL/
To be very good at or better than others.
"She excels in mathematics."
Persevere
/per-suh-VEER/
To keep trying even when things are difficult.
"He persevered and finally succeeded."
Knowledge
/NOL-ij/
Facts and skills gained through learning.
"Reading builds our knowledge."
Curious
/KYOOR-ee-us/
Eager to learn or know about something.
"The curious student asked many questions."
