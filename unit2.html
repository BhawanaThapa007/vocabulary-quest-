📚 Learn the Words
Read each word carefully. Click 🔊 Listen to hear how it sounds. Learn the meaning and example sentence.
Nurture
/NUR-cher/
Verb
To care for and encourage the growth of someone or something.
"Good parents nurture their children with love and patience."
Compassion
/kum-PASH-un/
Noun
A feeling of sympathy and concern for someone who is suffering.
"She showed great compassion by helping her injured classmate."
Cherish
/CHER-ish/
Verb
To hold something or someone very dear and important to your heart.
"We cherish every moment we spend with our grandparents."
Sibling
/SIB-ling/
Noun
A brother or sister.
"My sibling and I share a bedroom and often read together."
Ancestor
/AN-ses-ter/
Noun
A person in your family who lived a very long time ago.
"Our ancestors from Nepal built beautiful temples long ago."
Bond
/BOND/
Noun
A strong feeling of connection or friendship between people.
"The bond between twins is often very special and deep."
