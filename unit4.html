📝 Unit 4 Story — Community & Culture
Nepal is a land of amazing diversity where many different peoples, languages, and cultures live together in harmony. People cooperate every day in their communities — building schools together, cleaning rivers, and growing food. One important tradition is Dashain, where all family members return home to celebrate together. The old temples and festivals are part of Nepal's heritage that every citizen feels very proud of. Many young people choose to volunteer their time to help their communities. It is the responsibility of everyone to preserve these wonderful traditions for all future generations.
